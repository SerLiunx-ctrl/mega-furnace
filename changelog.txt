Version: 0.1.1
Date: 2026-03-28
  Features:
    - Added the High-Throughput Inserter as a late-game bulk logistics option for giant furnace setups.
    - Added localized mod title and description entries for in-game mod browsing.
  Graphics:
    - Reworked the High-Energy Mega Furnace visuals to better distinguish it from the lower-tier furnace.
    - Changed the High-Energy Mega Furnace working heater effect to a more distinct blue arc-flame style.
  Balancing:
    - Reduced the power usage of both giant furnaces to one quarter of their previous values.
    - Reduced the pollution output of both giant furnaces to half of their previous values.
    - Reduced the crafting cost of both giant furnaces to half of their previous values.
    - Reduced the High-Throughput Inserter crafting cost and power usage.
    - Made the High-Throughput Inserter hand-craftable.
    - Set the High-Throughput Inserter to a fixed 30-item pickup stack.
  Changes:
    - Restricted the giant furnace recipes to the custom assembling category used by Assembling Machine 3.
